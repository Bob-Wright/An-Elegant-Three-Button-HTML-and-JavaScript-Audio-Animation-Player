This panel also uses all three of the AVPlayer buttons and its object is just to play an audio track.<br>The first button controls audio muting and the default is muted audio. This button enables or disables the audio playback much like the familiar mute button.<br>The second button aids in the important area of content accessibility. It enables or disables the display of a &#34;closed captioning&#34; or text transcript/description of the audo content.<br>The third button is the PLAY button and it replays the audio without any animation content.
